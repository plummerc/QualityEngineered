Gray Box Testing

Continuing with the analogy of baking and consuming chocolate chip cookies, Gray Box Testing is most typically akin to the food critic's process for reviewing the end product.
The food critic has a deeper knowledge and understanding of the units, inputs, outputs, and background processes required to produce the cookie sitting in front of them.
This type of testing is often overlooked or taken for granted, but there is a lot to be said for having an expert with the technical and end product knowledge to provide a strong and thorough critique. Gray Box Testing lives at the heart of continuous improvement by combining the care for and understanding of background details with the end consumer's requirements.
